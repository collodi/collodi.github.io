Localization refers to the problem of pinpointing your location. The GPS is one of the most important technologies we have today. We can do thousands of different things by simply knowing where we are.

A similar logic applies to climbing. I imagine climber localization will have dozens of applications in the future. This topic deals with the localization technology itself, not the applications.

The topic will cover anything related to localization on a climbing wall. My vision for the topic is discussed in more detail in the first article.
