--- layout: page title: Better Brush ---

Climbing brushes are the reason we all send our projects. The brushes are important in competition climbing and pushing the limits outdoors. In this topic, we explore different approaches to brushing in climbing.

The motivation for the project was the COVID restriction on competition climbers to brush their own climbs. I thought there must be a more efficient way. Even though the restriction is lifted, I see a potential in this topic.

This is a fun side project for me, so I hope you enjoy it as well!
